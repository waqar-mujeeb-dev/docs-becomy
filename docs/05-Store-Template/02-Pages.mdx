# Managing Pages on Becomy

Becomy enables you to effortlessly create, customize, and manage your store pages through its user-friendly admin dashboard.

<img className="docs-ss" src={useBaseUrl('/img/pages-becomy.png')} />

## Setting Up Your Page

To begin creating a page, navigate to your **`Becomy Admin Dashboard`** and open the **`Store Template`** tab. Under this tab, select **`Pages`** to access the pages section.

## Creating a New Page

- **Create New Page**: In the Pages section, click on the "`Create New Page`" button located in the top-right corner. This will open a form that allows you to write, format, and edit your page content with ease.

---

This form allows you to create a new page for your store. Fill in the required fields such as:

- **Title**: The main heading or name of the page.
- **Page Title**: A secondary title displayed in the browser tab.
- **Page Description**: A brief summary or content description for the page.
- **Category**: Select the appropriate category (e.g., `landing_page`) to organize your page.

Once all fields are completed, click the "`Save`" button to create the page.

<img className="docs-ss" src={useBaseUrl('/img/create-new-page-becomy.png')} />

## Page Management Tools: Edit, Preview, and Delete

Once you’ve created page, managing them is straightforward from your Becomy dashboard.

- **Preview Page**: The preview feature lets you see how your page will look to visitors before it is published. It provides a precise visual display of the page’s content, including text, images, and layout. To preview the page, locate it in the "`Pages`" section and click on the "`Eye icon`".

<img className="docs-ss" src={useBaseUrl('/img/page-preview-becomy.png')} />

- **Deleting Page**: If you need to remove a page, locate the page in the "`Pages`" section and click on the `Delete icon`. A confirmation prompt will appear to ensure you wish to proceed. `Confirm the deletion` to permanently remove the page from your store.
