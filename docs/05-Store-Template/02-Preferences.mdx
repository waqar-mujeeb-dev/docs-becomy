# Preferences

The **`Preferences`** section in the `Store Template` tab allows you to customize essential details about your Becomy store, including `SEO settings`, URL, and integration with `analytics tools`. This section is designed to help you control how your store appears to customers and on search engines.

## Store Details

This area is where you can see key information about your store:

- **Name**: This is the `display name` of your store.  

- **Title**: A customizable title for your store, often displayed in the browser tab.  

- **URL**: The `unique URL` where customers can access your store.  

- **Country Code**: Specifies the primary `country` associated with your store.  


## Home Page Preferences

This section lets you control how your store appears on search engines, helping to improve your visibility.

- **SEO Title**: The title that appears in search engine results for your store's homepage.  
  **Example**: *Iphone*

- **SEO Description**: A brief description that appears alongside the title in search engine results.  
  **Example**: *SEO Description*

  <img className="docs-ss" src={useBaseUrl('/img/becomy-seo.png')} />

- **Online Store Image Preview**: An image that appears when sharing your store link on `social media` or other platforms.  
  - **Upload Instructions**: You can upload a `PNG`, `JPG`, or `GIF` file up to 10MB in size, or drag and drop your preferred image.

<img className="docs-ss" src={useBaseUrl('/img/becomy-preview.png')} />

## Sales Channels

You can expand your online presence by setting up different sales channels:

- **Edit Changes**: Customize and add sales channels that help you reach a `broader audience`.

- **Google Analytics Account**: Connect your store to `Google Analytics` to track and analyze traffic.  

- **Facebook Pixel**: Integrate with `Facebook Pixel` for tracking and analytics.  

- **Twitter**: Connect your `Twitter` account for tracking purposes.
