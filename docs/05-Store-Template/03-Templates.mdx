# Design

The **`Design`** section within the **`Store Template`** tab allows users to choose and customize the design of their Becomy store. Similar to Shopify, this area provides various `template options` and design flexibility to help users create a visually appealing store.

## Template Store

<img className="docs-ss" src={useBaseUrl('/img/becomy-template.png')} />

This page enables users to browse and select from a variety of templates for their store design. Users can also upload their own templates if they prefer a custom design.

- **Current Design**: Displays the currently selected template for the store.  
  **Example**: *Becomy*

- **Browse Templates**: Allows users to explore available templates within the template library to find one that matches their store's style and branding needs.

<img className="docs-ss" src={useBaseUrl('/img/becomy-template-browse.png')} />

- **Saved Collection Templates**: Displays any templates that the user has `saved` for easy access and future use.  
 

  <img className="docs-ss" src={useBaseUrl('/img/becomy-save-template.png')} />

## Template Management

- **Upload Templates**: Users have the option to `upload custom templates` if they have a specific design they wish to use for their store.

- **Preview**: Once a template is selected, users can `preview` how their store will look with the chosen design.
