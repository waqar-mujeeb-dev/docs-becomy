## Paying Bills

This page provides information to help you understand and manage bill payments within your `Becomy store`. It covers different payment methods, including general bill payments and Becomy Shipping billing.

### Options for Paying Bills on Becomy

You can manage different types of bills directly through your Becomy admin, and each bill may have slightly different payment options:

- **General bill payments**: For general Becomy bills, such as paying for your subscription, you have multiple payment methods to choose from. These include `credit cards`, `Apple Pay`, and `PayPal`. The available payment methods may vary depending on your region.

- **Shipping billing**: Shipping label charges and adjustments are billed to the credit card associated with your Becomy account. All shipping charges are billed in the applicable currency. If you're specifically referring to paying for shipping label charges, these will be billed separately based on the shipping services used.

### Paying Your Becomy Bills

Your bills are paid using your primary payment method, which can include the following options:

- `Credit card`
- `Apple Pay`
- `PayPal`

Be sure to review your billing information to ensure that your payments are processed smoothly.
